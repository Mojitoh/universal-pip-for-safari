# Privacy Policy

Universal PiP for Safari does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The Safari extension runs locally in Safari to detect HTML5 video elements and add a Picture-in-Picture control. It does not send browsing history, page content, video titles, account data, or usage analytics to any server.

Settings such as whether the inline button is enabled are stored locally by Safari extension storage on the user's Mac.

If you contact the maintainer through GitHub or Buy Me a Coffee, those services may process information according to their own privacy policies.
